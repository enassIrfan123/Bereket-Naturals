Technical Benchmark

Nutribond™ Technology

What it is: A proprietary nutrient-binding process developed by Bereket Naturals to ensure that the delicate vitamins and minerals found in raw grains are preserved throughout the production cycle.

Why we need it: Standard high-heat processing often destroys essential nutrients. Nutribond™ bridges the gap, ensuring that every bite is as bioavailable as nature intended.

Our Purpose: We implemented this technology to fulfill our promise of providing "More Power To You," turning everyday meals into high-performance fuel for your body.

Safety Benchmark

DRAP Approvals

What it is: Certification by the Drug Regulatory Authority of Pakistan (DRAP), ensuring that our wellness products meet pharmaceutical-grade standards for safety and efficacy.

Why we need it: Digestive wellness is a serious responsibility. DRAP approval guarantees that products like Jhat Hazam are not just flavorful, but scientifically validated for consumer safety.

Our Purpose: Bereket Naturals pursued these rigorous certifications to build a foundation of medical-grade trust, ensuring that our traditional recipes meet modern global safety benchmarks.
