Our Legacy

Nourishing from the Roots

Bereket Naturals is born from a deep respect for the earth's bounty. We believe that true wellness begins with what we consume daily—from the comforting warmth of our morning porridge to the sustaining purity of our heritage rice.

Our commitment is simple: delivering uncompromised, wholesome goodness. Whether it's our slow-cooked organic jams, nutrient-dense cereals, or our carefully formulated digestive wellness blends, every product is a testament to natural vitality and sleek, modern living.

Our Products

What We Do

Whole Grain Cereals

Cereal grain-based food products are an integral part of global diets, providing essential vitamins, minerals, and proteins. At Bereket Naturals, one of Pakistan's premier cereal exporters, we specialize in high-quality, multi-nutrient grains that are cultivated with care to ensure they deliver the necessary energy to fuel your busy day.

We take immense pride in our leading cereal brands, Liffest, kuVvet, and Major Grains, which are renowned for their purity and hygienic processing. Whether it's cholesterol-lowering oats or fiber-rich grains, our reputable selection is designed to satisfy the diverse nutritional needs of our global customers.

Digestive Wellness

Experience "Flavors That Redefine Health" with Jhat Hazam, our signature line of premium digestive tablets designed to restore your inner balance. Crafted with the finest Himalayan Salt, these tablets provide sudden relief while delighting your palate with traditional flavors. At Bereket Naturals, we believe that digestive health should be a pleasant experience.

Choose from our unique flavor profiles including Anaar, Imlee, Aamchoor, Cola, and Amrood, each formulated to aid digestion effectively. At Bereket Naturals, we prioritize pure, bioactive ingredients to ensure your everyday comfort and health are never compromised.

Pulses & Rice

Niwala Pulses & Rice is a brand dedicated to a healthy lifestyle, rooted deeply in the rich agricultural heritage of Pakistan where every grain is a testament to quality. Our 100% natural and farm-sourced products are carefully selected to ensure that every meal is a celebration of purity, living up to our promise: "Har Niwala Khushyoun Wala".

Our premium range includes high-quality pulses such as Golden Kala Chana, Sabat Mash, Sabat Moong, and Kabuli Chana, alongside world-class rice varieties like Royal Kainat Basmati and Golden Sella. Sustainably grown and direct from selected farms, Niwala brings the finest Pakistani textures and aromas to your family table.

Organic Jams

Discover "Love & Happiness Preserved" with Jarfull, our premium collection of fruit preserves that capture the essence of sun-ripened orchards. We believe that jam should taste exactly like nature intended, which is why we slowly simmer our preserves using traditional methods to maintain the vibrant, natural flavor of the fruit.

Our delightful range features classic flavors like Strawberry Jam, Apple Jam, Mango Jam, and our signature Mix Fruit Jam, all made with natural fruit pectins. Each jar is free from commercial preservatives and unwanted fillers, ensuring a pure and joyful experience with every spoonful.

Nourishing Porridge

Empower your mornings with kuVvet porridge, deliverng "More Power To You" through our premium Rolled & Flaked whole grains. Specially formulated with Nutribond technology, our porridge is a comforting, vitamin-packed meal designed to support a health-conscious and productive lifestyle.

Choose between our 100% whole grain Barley Porridge and Wheat Porridge, both crafted to provide a profound bowl of warmth and nourishment. Thoughtfully recreated for modern wellness, kuVvet provides the high-quality fiber and essential nutrients your body needs to thrive from the very first bite.
